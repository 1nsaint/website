WiFi PC Remote

Control your PC from the couch. WiFi PC Remote turns your Android device into a full keyboard, touchpad, and media controller with customizable colors, quick-access panels, and real-time PC stats. Instantly type symbols, send media keys, switch layouts, or monitor CPU, RAM, and disk usage without touching the physical machine.

Why you’ll love it

Instant typing

Every character you type on your phone appears instantly on your PC, including emojis and special symbols.

Fluid touchpad

Swipe to move the cursor, tap to click, and use two fingers to scroll. Low-latency, high-precision control across your WiFi.

Media & system keys

Volume, play/pause, Escape, Tab, Windows key, and more—right from your handset.

No cloud required

Connections happen on your local network only. Your keystrokes stay private and fast.

Get started in minutes

  1. Download and install the Windows helper from the button above.
  2. Launch the Android app, connect to your PC on the same WiFi network.
  3. Type, swipe, and control playback instantly.
